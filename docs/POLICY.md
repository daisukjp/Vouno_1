# POLICY — User-Generated Content (MVP)

**Product:** Visual Beta Cards for Climbing  
**Scope:** MVP only (local data, no cloud sync)

## Summary

This app lets users create “beta cards” by marking holds on their own climbing photos. All media stays on the device unless the user explicitly exports/shares it.

## User Ownership Requirement

- You must own the photo or have clear permission to use it.
- Do not upload or mark photos you do not have rights to use.

## Privacy & Background Guidance

- Avoid including faces or identifiable people in photos.
- Avoid capturing personal items, signage, or private/identifiable details in the background.
- If a photo includes other people, get their permission before using or sharing it.

## Gym-Sensitive Boundaries

- Respect gym policies and staff instructions about photography.
- MVP does **not** build or store any official gym wall database.
- Do not use the app to systematically document or recreate a gym’s proprietary routes or setting information.

## Data Handling (MVP)

- Media and marks remain on the device unless you explicitly export/share.
- Deleting a beta card removes its local data permanently; no recovery.

## Short In-App Warning Copy

- “Use only photos you own or have permission to use.”
- “Avoid faces and identifiable people or details.”
- “Respect gym policies; no official wall database in MVP.”
